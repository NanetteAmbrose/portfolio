Communication and Graphic Artworks

Feb 9, 2018| Category: typography| website: Themefisher

As an up and coming communications professional I have studied and done work in written and spoken communication, graphic design, public relations, and marketing. Here is some of my work:

Obituary

Obituary.pdf

I was assigned to write an obituary in the event of an important person’s death. I chose Mark Zuckerberg. I researched his life and found then-current information that made for a poignant story. I did my best to craft a picture of an industry tycoon, while still remembering his humanity.

Press Release

Press-Release.pdf

A mock press release I made for a class. We were given a set of facts about an event and then told to make a newsworthy press release. I made a point to be honest while still representing the fictional company. I kept in the vital information, while still painting the company in the best light.

Women in Video Games

Women-in-Video-games.pdf

A speech I wrote on the representation of women in media, specifically in video games. The goal was to make a well-researched speech that convinced the audience. I have always cared deeply about representation, especially in my favorite forms of media, so women in video games was an obvious choice for this project.

Public Relations Plan

Public-Relations-Plan.pdf

In collaboration with a group I created a public relations plan for one of the clubs or organizations that operated on the campus of the University of Saint Francis. One of my major roles was helping to work out the strategies and tactics used in our mission. I was unable to put the whole plan here, but I was able to add in our basic plan: our goal, objectives, strategies and tactics.

Brochure

Stickley-Brochure.pdf

I made this with Adobe InDesign and Photoshop; I was assigned to make a four-fold brochure as class work. For this design I wanted to make something warm that would make people think of home, yet also distinguished enough to give the feeling of high-end furniture. I went with an earthy brown and leaf design because their natural tones worked well with the color palette of the photos I had to work with, while also capturing the feeling I was looking for.

The brochure is meant to be folded inward at the center with the topside facing outward. The ends are meant to be folded in so when the brochure is first opened they form one picture. The inner side, seen on the bottom of the pdf, is meant to opened and viewed as one page.

Logos

Logos.pdf

These logos were created in a class working with the Adobe Suite, specifically Adobe Illustrator. Graphic design is not my specialty, but I do understand how to use the Adobe Suite. This project was a pleasure to make.

Vestor Art

Vector Art.pdf

This self-portrait was created in a class working with the Adobe Suite. My goal was to not only capture my face with vector art, but also to serve as a form of self expression.

Podcast

In the fall of my sophomore year for a speech class we were assigned to record a podcast with the help of music tech students. For this assignment, I conducted an interview of one of the cast members of a university play. I worked to coordinate with her and the music tech students to arrange the meeting time. The music tech students worked the recording equipment and edited the final project. I picked the topic, worked to arrange the interview, and picked the questions asked.
